Student FAQ

QUESTIONS NEW STUDENTS ASK FREQUENTLY

  • Assignments
    1. Is there a late policy?

      2. Late work will not be accepted unless provided with a doctors note for sickness or other personal reasons.

    2. What is the total weightage of assignments?

      3. The total weight of assignments is 15% our of your total grade.

    1. Will there be religious accommodations?

      2. Accommodations will be made, given a one week notice.

    2. Will exams be open book?

      3. No, exams will not be open book

  • Grading
    1. Will grades be on a curve?

      2. No, grades will not be on a curve
